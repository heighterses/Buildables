Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join your community?

You can join our community by signing up for events, participating in hackathons, or applying for our fellowship programs. Follow us on social media to stay updated.

Yes, we offer internship opportunities through our fellowship programs. Check our Fellowships page for more information and application details.

How can I sponsor an event?

We welcome partnerships and sponsorships. Please email us at partnerships@buildables.pk with your proposal.
