About Buildables

Empowering the next generation of innovators.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Buildables emerged from a vision to transform Pakistan's tech landscape by creating opportunities for aspiring developers, designers, and innovators. We believe in the power of community-driven learning and hands-on experience.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and industry requirements by providing practical training, mentorship, and real-world project experience to tech enthusiasts across Pakistan.
